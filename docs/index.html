Interview with confidence.

First job interviews can be scary. My personalized preparation gives you the confidence to land the right job for you.

We'll strategize the best way to highlight your skills and match them to employers' needs.

The confidence you gain goes well beyond the next interview. These are skills for life.

How it Works

1

Before we meet, send me your resume, plus job description for upcoming interview or ideal target job

2

I'll send you a list of commonly asked interview questions for you to review

3

We will discuss:

  • meeting and greeting
  • identifying your key messages
  • verbal and non-verbal communication
  • what to wear
  • job interview followup duties

4

In-person or video mock interview with immediate feedback

Why you may need interview coaching:

  • If you tend to get nervous, practice will help.
  • If you get interviews but no offers, I can help you see blind spots that interviewers won't tell you about.
  • Non-verbal communications: your facial and body language speak volumes even if you don't say a word.
